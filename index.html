With great happiness we look forward to gathering our dearest family and friends for a joyous celebration in the French countryside: afternoons in the gardens, candlelit suppers, and unhurried conversations beneath the trees. Below you will find a first glimpse of the festivities, how to reach us, and where we hope you will rest your head.

Location

Château de Garrevaques sits just beyond Toulouse, about an hour’s drive into the countryside. Once you leave the city, the road winds through fields and trees until it arrives at the château’s quiet outskirts.

Travel

Air & train

Most guests will travel via Toulouse (TLS), with Carcassonne (CCF) and Castres–Mazamet (DCM) as charming alternatives. High-speed SNCF/TGV trains serve Toulouse; from there a hired car or arranged transfer will bring you to the château.

Local transfers

We will arrange a shared shuttle between town and château; fuller details will arrive with a later RSVP.

Dress Code

We kindly request cocktail attire in our suggested palette. Please refrain from wearing full black, white, red, or cream, as those hues are reserved for the wedding party. A colour inspiration and further recommendations will be shared in our FAQs.

Children & Plus Ones

With heartfelt thanks for your understanding, we can only host the guests named on this invitation. If you have any questions, please reach out to us directly.

Accommodation

Hôtellerie du Lac

We have set aside 24 rooms at Hôtellerie du Lac, a small lakeside inn around fifteen minutes from the château, including an accessible room and several family suites. The hotel has created a wedding package for us, which is expected to come to roughly €40–45 per guest, per night, breakfast included, depending on how rooms are shared.
